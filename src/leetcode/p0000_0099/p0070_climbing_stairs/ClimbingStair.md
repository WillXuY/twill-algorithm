## Idea 2
1. List add the combination of steps.
2. Permutation with repetition [Search for help](https://brilliant.org/wiki/permutations-with-repetition/)

$$
\frac{n!}{n_1!n_2!n_3!\cdots n_k!}
$$

### in this case: 
- n = input - n2
- n1 = input - n2 * 2

$$
\frac{(n - n_2)!}{n_2!\times (n - n_2 * 2)!}
$$
